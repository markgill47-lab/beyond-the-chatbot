Visual Direction — Slide Mockups

Same slide ("What Is an Agent?"), three identities. In each: toggle light/dark (top-right), flip narration mode, click a diagram node to open a modal with the narration play button, and check the lower-third nav. Judge them projected and in-browser.

Mockup A Recommended

Editorial × Cartographic

Magazine typography (Fraunces + Space Grotesk) over a grid, with map-language diagrams — contour rings, graticule ticks, legend labels — and a survey palette. Distinctive, on-theme for a GIS room, great projected.
Mockup B

Swiss Editorial

Stripped-back international-typographic style: huge Archivo type, an oversized index numeral, rules instead of boxes, one signal-red accent, lots of air. Timeless and premium; less field-specific.
Mockup C

Cartographic / Atlas

The full map metaphor: each slide is a plate with a neatline, coordinate ticks, scale bar, north arrow, and legend. The agent diagram becomes a surveyed region with a summit core. Boldest and most thematic.
